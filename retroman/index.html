Welcome to Retroman

Play The Game

Description

Retroman is a retro arcade game built in HTML5 and JavaScript, paying homage to the timeless classic, Pac-Man. Navigate a maze, eat all the pellets, and avoid the mischievous ghosts. Turn the tables by grabbing a power pellet and chase them down for bonus points!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to guide Retroman through the maze.

  • Up Arrow: Move Up
  • Down Arrow: Move Down
  • Left Arrow: Move Left
  • Right Arrow: Move Right

You can press the next direction key just before reaching a turn to make smoother corners!

Core Features

  • Four Unique Ghosts: Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde each have their own AI for chasing you down.
  • Power Pellets: Eating these larger pellets will make the ghosts vulnerable for a short time.
  • Bonus Fruit: A cherry will periodically appear in the maze for extra points.
  • Special Power-Ups: Find the Speed Boost to move faster or the Freeze Clock to stop ghosts in their tracks.
  • CRT Effects: The game features authentic scanlines and glow effects to emulate a classic arcade monitor.

Setup & Customization

To hear the sound effects, place your .wav audio files in the same folder as the index.html game file. The game looks for these specific filenames:

  • beginning.wav
  • music.wav
  • pellet.wav
  • power_pellet.wav
  • eat_ghost.wav
  • death.wav
  • fruit.wav
  • powerup.wav
